Cai, et al. Climatic stability and geological history shape global centers of neo- and paleoendemism in seed plants. Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci. U.S.A., 2023.

Below, the content of each folder (name to the left) is described (text to the right):
#----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------#
Data:  Data.Rdata, contains phylogenetic metrics based on the dataset excluding apomictic taxa and without (matched phylogeny) and with (merged phylogeny) missing species added to the phylogeny, and environmental variables for each region. 
       NOTE: Please use the "ref_ID" in this dataset to download checklists of each region used in the study from GIFT.

Code:   R code used in the publication.
